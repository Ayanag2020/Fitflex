LUNGE

Description :
A lunge can refer to any position of human body where one leg is postioned forward with knee bent and foot flat on the ground while the other leg is positioned behind.

PUSH UP

Description :
By raising and lowering the body the arms, push-ups exercise the pectoral muscles, triceps, and anterior deltoids, with ancillary benefits to the rest of the deltoids, serratus anterior, coracobrachialis and the midsection as a whole.

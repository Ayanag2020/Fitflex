GET TO KNOW YOUR BODY MASS INDEX

your body mass index, or BMI is a measure of your body fat levels based on your height and weight. It's recommended that adults have a BMI of approximately 18.5 to 24.9. the formola uses your weight (in KG or pounds) and your height (in meters or inches) to form a simple calculation that measures your body fat.

BMI FORMULA:

  • METRIC WAY

    BMI = weight(KG)/ (height (M))2

  • IMPERICAL WAY

    BMI = 703* weight(lbs) / (height (in))2
